Crowds' Favorite Local Brand

Careline
Known for trendy, affordable makeup aimed at teens and young adults, Careline's Oil Control Blush and Matte Liquid Lipsticks are popular for their quality and price.
Everbilena
EB is a crowd favorite because it offers a wide variety of high-quality makeup at affordable prices. Its accessibility, extensive product range, and popularity among different age groups, especially in the Philippines, make it a go-to brand for many.
Vice Cosmetics
Created by Filipino comedian Vice Ganda, Vice Cosmetics offers affordable, high-quality makeup, including the popular Kiss Matte lipsticks. The brand is known for vibrant colors and inclusivity, catering to all skin tones.
